Creating a comprehensive booking system

Challenge

Canterbury Travel, a distinguished multi-award-winning tour operator specializing in enchanting Lapland holidays, approached us with the need to develop a comprehensive back-office system tailored to their unique business requirements. The client shared their vision and ideas for a future system that could streamline their operations and enhance efficiency.

Solution

Embarking on a journey of close collaboration, we conducted a series of in-depth workshops to immerse ourselves in Canterbury Travelâs business model. This meticulous process allowed us to grasp every nuance of the operations we aimed to automate. The outcome of our collaborative endeavor is a robust, flexible back-office system that not only meets but exceeds the clientâs expectations. The system is now a cornerstone of their business, facilitating the travel of tens of thousands of people annually.

Key Features of the System:

  • Payment Processing: Integration with Barclays ePDQ and WorldPay, supporting multi-currency transactions.
  • Reservations Management: Streamlined handling of all aspects of travel reservations.
  • Tours Management: Efficient organization and management of various tours.
  • Itineraries Generation: Automated creation of detailed travel itineraries.
  • Bookkeeping: Comprehensive financial tracking and management.
  • Activities and Special Request Management: Tailored handling of customer requests and activity scheduling.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Seamless integration with external platforms for enhanced reporting capabilities.
  • Email Template Engine: Customizable email communication system.
  • Passenger Seats Allocation: Efficient allocation in coaches and flights.
  • Hotel Room and Cabin Allocation: Automated management of accommodation reservations.
  • Dining and Restaurant Allocation: Streamlined assignment of dining options.
  • Flight Ticketing: Coordination with Aircraft chartering companies, including reporting.
  • Online Check-In Facility: Convenient online check-in process for travelers.
  • Booking Pattern Analytics: Insightful data analysis to optimize booking trends.

Staff Portal

Challenge

Client was looking for ways to optimise hiring process, reflected in simple applications helping to allow hiring about 280 of seasonal personnel in a short period of time.

Solution

Was implemented simple and straightforward interface allowing to collect necessary data for hiring and process interview and auditions, with generation employment contracts for successful applicants.
Main features:

  • Email templating engine
  • Employee personal page where person can see their employments, work related information and contract.
  • Messaging system that keeps attached records to contract
  • Reporting system for accounting and for management
  • Flexible contract template management system, allowing to hire multiple roles
  • Permission system implementing fine-grain access for personal with different level of access to personal data

Advanced Passenger Information

Challenge

Client is tour operator that specialises in holidays to Finish Lapland. By UK aviation rules, all passenger on the flights required to present their passport information before the flight. Client wants to have a website where it will be presented passenger information and documents related to trip. Solution

Web application with adaptive interface (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop). Have integration with other third parties for payments, document generations and secure data storage for personal information.
